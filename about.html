About

This website contains two games

1.Complete The Lyrics

Type lyrics from any line in the song.The lines following each other don't need to be consecutive.

After Completing You can check your score.Once you check your score the next button will be enabled for the next level

(You should not use any punctuation mark other than "full stop(.)")

2.Guess The Song

Type the Song name followed by Artist name in the format "Track Name-Artist Name".

After Completing You can check your score.Once you check your score the next button will be enabled for the next level
